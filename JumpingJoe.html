Jumping Joe

No risk, no cash!

To prevent hunger, Joe needs to eat cherries in order to survive . Please help the cute Joe by running and jumping
across the level. Joe like to eat cherries but you have to overcome many obstacles like mushroom and obstacles
such as rocks and trees that prevent Joe from eating his cherries. If you begin to play it, you will be addicted to it and
fully immersed into the game environment.
But be careful, not only good things are flying through the air.
This game can be played by any user of any age.
